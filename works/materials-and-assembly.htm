Materials and assembly

HOW THINGS COME TOGETHER

Class Date: 2016 Spring | Individual

ASSIGNMENT 1: ASSEMBLY

The first assignment focused on reconstruct a given section, and make axon assembly drawings. Through this assignment, I gained a better understanding of a traditional concrete-steel composite structure, and I became familiar with drawing conventions.

ASSIGNMENT 2: CODE REVIEW

For the second assignment, we needed to look into IBC 2012's occupancy and egress session. We used Corbusier's Heidi Weber Museum as a case study. We reviewed the occupancy of spaces and designed new egress that would meet the modern code.

ASSIGNMENT 3: MATERIAL

The third assignment required research into Cross-Laminated Timber Construction, including material properties, utilities, etc. We then needed to show how a CLT construction is assembled.

ASSIGNMENT 4: SYNTHESIS

The last assignment integrates with the studio project. We need to do code review on our own design, and then draw a critical section of the design.
