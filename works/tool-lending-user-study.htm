Tool Lending

THE CURRENT SCENE ON
TOOL LENDING AND SHARING

Project Date: 2018 Spring | Collaborative

INTENT

The intent for this research is to gain some quick and basic understanding for a certain market, and to provide insights for further design opportunities. Another team in our class later on picked up our data and continued to design an app that addresses the issues found in the research. Actually, one of my other projects, eggschange "inherited" another group's research data for the mutual teaching market.

For this project, our team was qualitatively researching the supplies and demands in the current tool sharing scene. We started with competitive analysis, looking at precedent business models and examples for tool-sharing facilities. We also investigated and interviewed people from local tool-sharing libraries in Pittsburgh. We limited our scope to only research the potential need in an apartment environment, and we contacted apartment managers and interviewed different people who are living in apartments.
