Span

STRUCTURE, DESIGN AND FABRICATION

Project Date: 2015 Spring Studio | Collaborative

PROJECT

This is a project in my second semester. In this project, teams of 2 are asked to design and fabricate a structure that spans 4 feet. The final structure should withhold one adult person's weight. In a sense, this is my very first free design project.

At first, My teammate and I were building random ideas out of paper and cardboard. After we combined our ideas and continued brainstorming, we decided to carry on designing with the concept of first and secondary structure in mind. Later in a collaborative improvising build session, we stumbled on the idea of a helix.

As shown, we went through various iterations, and fabricated test pieces to test out the joints. In the final design, we adjusted the orientation of primary components to utilize the grain direction of wood for more strength. The final span could support the weight of two grown men.
