Samson Liu is a 5th-year architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University, minoring in Human Computer Interaction.

His education has prepared him for designing through various interfaces, from physical to virtual, analog to digital. He is interested in the possibilities of using virtual information to strengthen but also challenge one's spatial perception. Right now, He is developing an Augmented Reality app for CMU's School of Architecture. Concorrently, he is pursuing a year-long thesis about spatial design in VR, which will be on the website soon.

Samson uses his free time to pursue his passions. He taught himself several coding languages, various designing and modeling softwares, as well as digital music production.
